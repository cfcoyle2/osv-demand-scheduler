# FSV Operations Summary - Sparta OSV Boat Plan

**Date:** May 7, 2026  
**Subject:** Addition of Fast Supply Vessel (FSV) to Sparta 20K Boat Plan  
**Version:** 2.0 (Revised with FSV)

---

## Key Changes to Boat Plan

The Sparta OSV Boat Plan has been revised to include **one dedicated Fast Supply Vessel (FSV)** for weekly consumable runs throughout the 86-day operation.

---

## Updated Fleet Configuration

### Previous Plan (Version 1.0)
- 4 Primary OSVs
- 1 Standby OSV (overlap period)
- **Total:** 5 vessels

### Revised Plan (Version 2.0)
- 4 Primary OSVs (unchanged)
- 1 Standby OSV (overlap period, unchanged)
- **1 Fast Supply Vessel (FSV) - NEW**
- **Total:** 6 vessels

---

## FSV-1: Fast Supply Vessel

### Specifications
| Parameter | FSV | Standard OSV (Comparison) |
|-----------|-----|---------------------------|
| **Deck Capacity** | 2,500 sq ft | 5,000 sq ft |
| **Liquid Capacity** | 500 bbls | 7,000-8,000 bbls |
| **Transit Time** | 24-28 hours | 36 hours |
| **Loading Time** | 48 hours | 72 hours |
| **Offloading Time** | 6-8 hours | 12-18 hours |
| **Full Cycle** | 4-5 days | 6-7 days |
| **Day Rate** | $8,000-$12,000 | $15,000-$25,000 |

### Primary Mission
- **Weekly consumables delivery** (12 total trips)
- **Urgent supply response** capability
- **Personnel transfer** support (if equipped)
- **Reduces load** on primary OSVs for small cargo

   - Office supplies

## Benefits of FSV Addition

### Operational Benefits
1. ✅ **Regular Supply Chain:** Weekly runs prevent shortages
2. ✅ **Reduced Emergency Costs:** FSV can handle urgent needs without chartering spot vessels
3. ✅ **OSV Efficiency:** Frees up primary OSVs for bulk cargo only
4. ✅ **Faster Response:** 24-28h transit vs 36h for emergency supplies
5. ✅ **Flexibility:** Schedule can adjust to operational needs
6. ✅ **Crew Support:** Better morale with regular fresh supplies

### Cost Benefits
1. ✅ **Lower day rate:** $8K-$12K vs $15K-$25K for OSV
2. ✅ **Prevents spot charters:** Emergency vessel charters cost $30K-$50K+ per trip
3. ✅ **Optimizes OSVs:** Large vessels focus on bulk/heavy cargo only
4. ✅ **Reduces waste:** Smaller loads prevent over-ordering

### Risk Mitigation
1. ✅ **Supply continuity:** Weekly runs reduce inventory risk
2. ✅ **Emergency backup:** FSV available for urgent needs
3. ✅ **Weather flexibility:** Smaller vessel can run in better weather windows
4. ✅ **Personnel transport:** Can assist with crew changes if needed

## Recommendations

### Primary Recommendation
✅ **PROCEED with FSV addition** to the Sparta boat plan

### Justification
1. **Cost is justified:** 10-12% increase provides significant operational benefits
2. **Risk reduction:** Weekly runs and emergency response capability
3. **Industry standard:** Most 80+ day programs utilize FSV support
4. **Crew welfare:** Regular fresh supplies improve morale and safety
5. **Efficiency:** Optimizes use of larger OSVs for bulk cargo

### Alternative Options (Not Recommended)
1. ❌ **No FSV:** Rely on OSV-4 for all consumables (higher risk, less frequent)
2. ❌ **Bi-weekly FSV:** Runs every 14 days (cost savings minimal, risk increases)
3. ❌ **Spot charter FSV:** Charter as-needed (higher cost, unreliable availability)
